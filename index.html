Game Rules

The Colonel Blotto game is a classic zero-sum conflict.

  • You (Player 1) must allocate exactly **100 soldiers** across **5 battlefields**.
  • The Enemy AI (Player 2) has also allocated 100 soldiers in secret.
  • On each battlefield, the player who allocates **more soldiers wins 1 point** for that field.
  • If the allocations are equal, it's a **draw (0 points)**.
  • The player with the most battlefield wins takes the game!
